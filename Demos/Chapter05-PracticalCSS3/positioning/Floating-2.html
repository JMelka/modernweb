Whilst you're looking at the differences, note that Internet Explorer has removed the margin from the top and bottom paragraphs in the right-hand div tag (these are not meeting margins, it is a margin meeting the padding. IE has left the margins in when two paragraphs meet, it's just removed the top and bottom margins. Similarly, it's done the same for the <ul> in the left hand div. Whilst this may be at first glance useful, it is not standards-compliant behaviour and thus shouldn't happen.
